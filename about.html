About AUSTDC

At AUST Debate Club, we believe in the power of dialogue to shape perspectives, challenge assumptions, and inspire change.
Whether you're a seasoned debater or just dipping your toes into the world of rhetoric,
our inclusive environment welcomes all skill levels and backgrounds.
Through regular meetings, workshops, and competitions, we provide opportunities
for members to sharpen their argumentation skills,
expand their knowledge on diverse topics,
and build confidence in expressing their viewpoints.
Join us at the AUST Debate Club and embark on a journey of self-discovery, growth, and meaningful connection.
